ABOUT LITTLE ANGLES (NGO)

Project Little Angle was initiated in 2016 by the students of Gapnat university of Computer science department specialisation in cloud based aplicatons with the aim of providing primary education to underprivileged girl children in India.

As prime minister of India Sri Narendra Modi has launched the digital India a nationwide movement. We are contributing in his vast vision by developing this NGO website. Technology is changing rapidly daily as more upgraded gadgets and softwares launch. If the poor people are not enlighten by education they will never be able to have a happy life. Itâ€™s a fact that the poor girls in India are discriminated most. We will try to improve their lifestyle with help of technology.

This website helps n connecting the volunteers, valuable contributors of the society to little girls who are not getting proper education with latest technologies. We will provide a platform to teachers and preachers.

Educating women would not only contribute to the economy but also cures various issues like population, pollution and social evils like the dowry system and child marriage would reduce as more women are educated.

Studies conducted in developing countries have shown that this is true. Today, even the World Bank has acknowledged that there is no investment more effective for achieving the millennium development goals than educating girls. "According to the World Bank, some of the benefits associated with girls' education include reduction of child and maternal mortality, improvement of child nutrition and health, lower fertility rates and improvement in economic production".

This project was designed as a sponsorship support programme which allows individuals to participate and support the technical education of a girl child in India.


Partners In The Cause

The project Little Angles is managed by the students of Gapat university persuing B.Tech in Computer science department (Cloud Computing) in assosiation with IBM. The project provides academic, material, social support and technology skills that allows a girl child to access quality education, attend school with dignity and reduces the chances of her dropping out.
